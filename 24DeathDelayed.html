[Death Delayed for a Short Time Because of the Will]

Synopsis:

Chaeyoon, once a famous hero, now lives as an unknown hunter, fulfilling the odd dying wishes of ex-colleagues before he can end his own life. Haunted by the past and drawn to Jihan, a skilled hunter he meets, Chaeyoon questions whether fulfilling last wishes and finding his own solace is possible. [Batoto]

Personal Review:

This BL hits different man. The artstyle is pretty good, and the story hits my kokoro. The main character feels both strong and fragile, and the flashbacks being comedic makes the serious moments hit you like a whiplash. It's really good, please try it!
