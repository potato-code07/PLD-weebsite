Personal Review(Spoilers!):

This anime is really refreshing. Like, super refreshing, a breath of fresh air from all the romance animes out there. It is really unique, and the characters are also interesting. The main character feels like your typical country bumpkin who knows nothing about the city, and perhaps that's what's refreshing about her. She has her own motivations and doesn't feel like a self-insert, and the way people interact is really...organic? Her interactions with Shima is also very cute. Shima himself seems to be written as a cool character whose mind you can't read, but throughout the show you will be shown how he's not written like an invincible ML. The manga is also really good, and the progression just gets better man.

Really refreshing anime, please give it a watch!
