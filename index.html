What is Anime?

Anime is a style of animation originating in Japan that is characterized by stark colorful graphics depicting vibrant characters in action-filled plots often with fantastic or futuristic themes.

What is Manga?

Manga is a style of Japanese comic books and graphic novels, typically aimed at adults as well as children. A manhwa is the korean version of this.

What is a Webnovel?

A form of novel written online, or on the "web".

Why choose Anime?

I've always loved wathching anime. My brother introduced it to me with the first ever anime I've ever watched: Shakugan No Shana. Since then, I've dug and dug into several animes, junkfood and masterpieces alike. Now, I don't remember like half of them, and I've stopped digging around and only watch mainstream anime.

Now, I'm more into reading Webnovels and reading Manhwas and Mangas, only occasionally checking in for interesting animes for the season. Still, I find myself rewatching some anime I liked. Even some trashy isekais. I love trashy isekais.

TLDR: I just really like anime, man.
