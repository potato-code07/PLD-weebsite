Personal Review(Spoilers!):

This is a really unique isekai anime. Not in an "I got reincarnated as a refridgerator" kind of way, or a "I got reincarnated with my smartphone" kinda way. It's really chill, and the world-building is really solid. Characters have unique motivations, and it really feels like a game. It's different from SAO, which has similar themes of being stuck in a game. I think it does it better, cuz character classes and different guilds raiding dungeons aren't really much explored in SAO.

The main character is also really cool, and there's a mystery to his past with his former guild. His former guild name is also really cool. It feels like the author really thought this through. Top tier anime. Now that I think about it, maybe I'd like to rewatch this this christmas.
